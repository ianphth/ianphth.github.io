About me

Sharing and witnessing every enthusiasm in the world and
design game as a phantom: knowing it's not there but yet able to affect players in various ways.

I am Ian Hsieh, the author of this site, alias is ianphth. Born and grown in Taiwan. An interaction designer/programmer. Plays basketball, alto Sax and piano, loves string music and a good story .

I've been playing all kind of video games and reading all sorts of stories for years. Mystery and twisting plots are the main things that attract me. So my emphasis will often be on storytelling and world design. Game music is also an aspect I try to mend into games a lot. Every single melody has different power. Looking forward to seeing the impact when these three elements bring into full play.

This site will be showcasing some of my works and sharing my thoughts toward life and work. If you are interested in anything in this site, please feel free to contact me. See the Contact page to learn more.
