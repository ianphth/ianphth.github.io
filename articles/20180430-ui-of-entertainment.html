User Interface of Entertainment (娯楽のUI)

"UI Crunch", which is a Japanese UI/UX organization, invited Nintendo developers to talk about their experience in this field. Take a look!

最近在日本的 UI/UX 推廣團體「UI Crunch」邀請了任天堂來作 UI/UX 的演講，內容算是相當淺顯易懂，有興趣的各位可以參考看看。這篇文章的內容主要是改寫「あのイベントが娯楽のUIだった(UI Crunch13 by任天堂レポ) ~前編~ 和 ~後編~ 」這兩個網頁的紀錄文章，有錯誤的話也請大家不吝指教。

Outlines: Playing heart and UI , Purpose of Splatoon UI, Conclusion

Playing heart and UI

玩心與使用者介面

The first talker is the chief UI/UX designer, Masaki Yoshifumi. As the beginning of his talk, he asked whether if everyone knows the meaning of UI.

第一位請到了擔任 UI/UX 設計總監的 正木義文 先生，作為這個部分的起頭，他問了大家一個簡單的問題： UI 的全名是甚麼呢？

Wait a minute! We only had five seconds to check the answers! Then he started to ask "Anyone goes for answer No.1?", "Anyone goes for answer No.2?". It was just chaotic and confusing.

看來我們在現場只有五秒多的時間去找到答案，然後正木先生便開始問大家「覺得是一號答案的請舉手」、「二號呢？」，一切都非常匆忙。

Obviously, it's non-sense to get the answer in such short time. The answer is No.58.

很明顯我們無法在這麼短的時間內找到 58 號這個答案。

However, here is the explanation of this quiz. "It's very likely that everyone has known the answer after seeing the question. But even if you know the answer, it's so difficult to find the answer in the information of this volume in 5 seconds."

而正木先生對這個流程作出了一個解釋：「大家想必在看到題目後便知道答案了吧。但要在這個資訊量尋找到正確的答案，五秒內是很困難的。」

In conclusion, the intent of this quiz method doesn't meet the concept of UI. We probably imagine the quiz in this way...

也就是說，這樣的出題方式的用意並不符合使用者介面的初衷。我們也許對於這個問題的預先期待是長這個樣子的...

Experiencing the example of misconept in UI will be an element of study, which can be an inner impact. This would remind us when we want to express something to our customers.

體驗這樣不符合 UI 規範的實際例子，可以作為能被有效內化的研修素材。這將會成為我們在與顧客溝通時會放在心上的重要概念。

Let's sum up the points of way to express:

1. Instead of teaching, make them experience
We should let users try instead of put ourselves in users' shoes.

2. The first-time experience is important
We only have one first-time. The time you feel the freshness is the time boredom starts to grow. It's like the happiness of wearing new shoes will slowly wear off.

3. The experience should be interesting
When you're moved, the next feeling will come right after that. We should create the surprising experience through our product.

這邊總結一下向使用者傳達訊息的幾個要點：

1. 教導 →體驗
我們應該讓使用者自己去嘗試及體驗，並不是要把自己放在使用者的角度去思考。

2. 第一次的體驗很貴重
第一次的體驗只有一次，當開始感到新鮮的時候，也是無趣感開始萌芽的時刻。這就像是第一次穿鞋的開心感慢慢流逝的感覺。

3. 體驗還是要以有趣為主
已經萌發的感情會像漣漪一樣觸動下一個感情。我們應該透過商品去傳達驚喜的體驗。

Next, he talked about Mario Maker which is an user-editable game. The main two aims are "easy to realize" and "high functionality".

接下來，正木先生提到了玩家可以自行編輯關卡的遊戲 Mario Maker 。主要的兩個製作目標為「易用性」及「高機能」。

The approaches to make these two aims eligible are to let user feel "It's awesome to be able to do so many things!" instead of "It's so hard to do so many things.". And to make surrounding people feel impressive and happy. You can see the high responsiveness in every control and the ability to visualize on bigger screen(TV). Sharing it with your friends and family will empower the fun.

有兩種思路可以讓這兩個目標被實現，第一個是要讓使用者感覺「可以作這麼多事情好像很厲害！」而不是「這麼多事情我能做到嗎」，另一個則是要讓其他觀者也能感到開心。 你可以看到在裡面每個操作都具有強回應性(每個動作必然都有明顯的結果)，以及連上大電視後與他人分享的過程。和家人及朋友分享快樂，能讓快樂更膨脹。

In this paragraph, we know the points to design UI to express the playing heart:

1. UI-wise design and entertainment-wise design complement with each other
If we adopt the UI-wise design methodology, ideas will be limited. If we adopt the entertainment-wise design methodology, we'll fall for the limitation of ideas. Before reviewing the design, we should not count the UI out of the entertainment field. Thinking about balance is crucial.

2. Use entertainment-wise design to transform weakness into strength
It's about the process of transforming the trivial things into entertainment. For example, your kid walks slowly because he/she wants to go to restroom. At that time, you can say "Let's race!" and your kid would be happy to do so. By watching your kid running, you'll feel the growth of your own child, like "He runs faster than before...". Eventually you feel the happiness, too.

3. 3 seats for Shou-gi, 5 seats for Mah-jong (Shou-gi needs 2 people to play and Mah-jong needs 4)
Not only the people who involve in the product have the experience. The surroundings of product should be considered an all-around entertainment experience. Sharing the happiness will amplify the happiness.

在這邊總結一下傳達玩心的 UI 設計有甚麼要點：

1. UI 角度和娛樂角度具有兩人三腳的互補特性
如果只用 UI 的角度去設計，點子將會被侷限住。如果只用娛樂的角度去設計，將會太注重在完成點子本身。 在進行討論前，如果將此視為和娛樂無關的範疇將會很可惜，重要的是必須思考平衡性。

2. 利用娛樂角度將缺點變成優點
試著將麻煩及無聊的事利用這個機會轉變成娛樂吧！例如說小朋友在想要去上廁所時，走路會變得緩慢。在這種時候跟他說「我們比賽跑到那邊吧！」小朋友將會非常開心的跑起來。 而在看著小朋友跑步的途中自己也會感受到小朋友的成長，也變得開心起來。

3. 將棋三個位子、麻將五個位子
不只是接受到娛樂產品本身的人會有體驗，所有娛樂產品周圍的人事物都會成為整體的娛樂體驗，分享快樂可以將快樂增幅。

Purpose of Splatoon UI

Splatoon UI的目的

The second talker is UI/UX designer, Tachibana Mariko. The first thing she talked about was the aim of UI design in Splatoon: "Simple UI" and "Fresh design". Since Splatoon was a new title, they try to establish a fresh art style. But how to do it?

第二位講者是擔任 UI/UX 設計師的 橘磨理子 小姐，她提到的第一件事便是 Splatoon UI 的大方向：使用簡單以及新鮮設計。 在 Splatoon 還是嶄新 IP 的當時，她們嘗試建立起新鮮的美術風格。但要怎麼作呢？

The first thing in their mind is the FONT . But existing font didn't meet their needs. So they decided to make a new font. They didn't pay all the attention on expressing freshness. They made the new font readable for sure.

他們所想到第一個能創造新鮮感的元素便是字型。但現有字型它們並不滿意。所以她們決定作一組新字型。 這組新字型並不只是注重於新鮮感，她們也希望有最低程度的可讀性。

You can see the thickness of the new font. They focus on sporty, refreshing image. And their line was designed to be like liquid and organic things. So it's easy to tell the game title from the font.

可以看到他們在字體的粗細度上聚焦在活力及健康感，而線條則是具液體和有機物的印象。所以我們可以從字體辨別這是哪一款遊戲。
